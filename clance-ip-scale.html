Clance IP Scale

Context

The Impostor Phenomenon was defined by researchers Pauline Rose Clance and Suzanne Imes (Georgia State University). They developed this questionnaire (CIPS - Clance Impostor Phenomenon Scale) to help assess these feelings of self-doubt.

For each question, please select the number that best indicates how true the statement is of you. It is best to give the first response that enters your mind.

Scale: 1 (Not at all true) to 5 (Very true)
